---===Dentists and Dragons===---

VR Game where you are a dentist for dragons.

The dragon feels pain.

You loose when the dragon has too much pain(it snaps your head off).

Each level another dragon comes to you as a patient(randomize color/ears/horns/...).

You proceede to the next level once you fixed all the teeth.

You have different tools to your dispoal and teeth have different kinds of ailments.

Tools:

Drill
Brush
Waterpump
Dental Hook
Toothpick
Floss
Sword
Concrete
Crown(Wood/Gold)
Grills?
Magery?

Parameters:
Pain

Ailments:
